# Pricing Policy (v1, archived)

This is the prior pricing policy. Superseded by v2 — kept here so the retrieval evaluator can detect a designed conflict.

## Standard plans

- **Starter:** $29 / seat / month, 5 seat minimum.
- **Team:** $79 / seat / month, 10 seat minimum.

## Enterprise discount

The maximum enterprise discount under v1 is **15%**, available for customers committing to 24 months and 50+ seats.

## Pilot programme

Pilots run for 14 days. There is no free trial outside the pilot.

## Refund policy

Annual prepay customers may request a pro-rata refund within the first 30 days of the subscription start date.
