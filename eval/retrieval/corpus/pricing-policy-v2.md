# Pricing Policy (v2, current)

Current pricing policy. Replaces v1.

## Standard plans

- **Starter:** $29 / seat / month, 3 seat minimum.
- **Team:** $79 / seat / month, 10 seat minimum.
- **Business:** $149 / seat / month, 25 seat minimum.

## Enterprise discount

The maximum enterprise discount is **25%**, available for customers committing to 24 months and 100+ seats. Negotiated discounts above 25% require VP Sales approval.

## Pilot programme

Pilots run for 30 days, doubled from v1. Customers receive a single dedicated solutions engineer during the pilot.

## Refund policy

Annual prepay customers may request a pro-rata refund within the first 30 days of the subscription start date. Monthly customers may cancel at any time and are not refunded for the current billing period.

## Procurement contact

For RFPs and procurement questions, route to procurement@example.com.
