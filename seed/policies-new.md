# Vacation Policy (memo, Q1 2026)

Effective immediately, employees accrue **2.5 days per month** of paid
vacation. Carry-over into the following calendar year is capped at **30
days**, up from the previous 10. The accrual change applies retroactively
from January 1.

# Expense Reimbursement (revised)

Receipts must be submitted within 30 days via the finance portal.
Manager pre-approval is required for any single expense over **$1,000**
(previously $500).
