# Vacation Policy (handbook v3, 2025)

Vacation accrues at **1.5 days per month** for full-time employees. The
unused balance carries over to the next calendar year, capped at **10
days**. Anything above the cap is forfeited on January 1.

# Expense Reimbursement

Submit receipts within 30 days via the finance portal. Anything over
**$500** requires manager pre-approval before the expense is incurred.

# Onboarding (first week)

New employees receive a laptop, badge, and an introduction to the team
within their first week. The IT helpdesk is available 24/7 at extension
4357.
